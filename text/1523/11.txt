looks revitalised this
month, although ru-
mours say artificial
surgery was involved.

younger and rejuve-
nated this week and it
hasn't gone unnoticed.
Sources say the Beit
Quad is in a sour mood
after hearing about
its rival’s new look.
Prince's Gardens was
overheard yesterday
rustling, "I can't be-
lieve the cheek of the
QL! Not only has it had
a turf lift but I think it's
also had its tower en-
largened! Then again
I'm not surprised, she's
not exactly been fussy
about which marquees
she's laid.”
