Ground at Harlington are now certain to be exploited
during the eighties. When first discussed in FELIX No
547 (Friday 7th March), the question of extracting the
minerals from the college land was treated coolly by
Sherfield administration; but negotiations have now
reached an advanced stage and planning permission
seems to be a formality.
