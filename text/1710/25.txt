SLITHERLINK
Connect horizontally and vertically adjacent dots so that the lines form a
simple loop with no loose ends. The number inside a square represents how
many of its four sides are segments in the loop.
NONOGRAM
Colour in some of the squares to make a picture. Each number
represents a block of consecutive black squares within that row/col-
umn. Each block needs to be separated by at least one white square.
