NONOGRAM
Colour in some of the
squares to make a pic-
ture. Each number rep-
resents a block of con-
secutive black squares
within that row/column.
Each block needs to be
separated by at least one
white square.
CHESS

forced checkmate in four
moves.
SUDOKU
Fill all the boxes with a number be-
tween 1 and 9. A number can only
appear once in a row/colum, and
only once in each sub-box.
