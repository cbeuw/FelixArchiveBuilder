Election campaigning begins

Elections began in earnest on Monday,
with candidates for the available po-
sitions given the opportunity to show
students across Imperial their creden-
tials.
Wednesday saw the second-ever
‘Meet the Candidates’ live televised
hustings take place, broadcast by Im-
perial’s student television network
STOIC from the West Basement in
Beit Quad. The 80 minute broadcast
saw candidates, running for the sabbat-
ical positions, faculty union presiden-
tial positions and four student trustee
positions, take to the screen to deliver
their manifesto points and respond
to questions sent in by email or over
Twitter. Whereas last year’s broadcast
was not made available following the
event, this year’s is now available to

site. In addition, the live stream found
