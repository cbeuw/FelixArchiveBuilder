Neural Networks, Attachment
and Therapy
Thurs 8th Nov 6pm
LT Ground, Business School
Imperial College
Chaplaincy Centre
A conversation across the disciplines of computational
neurodynamics, psychology and religion.
Prof Abbas Edalat (Computing) will talk about his research on neural networks in
relation to attachment theory, a key element in human development. Reflection
on these ideas will be offered by psychotherapists from Buddhist and Christian
religious traditions, with audience Q & A..
