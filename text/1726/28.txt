form a simple loop with no loose ends.The number inside a square
represents how many of its four sides are segments in the loop.

NONOGRAM
Colour in some of the squares to make a picture. Each number represents a
block of consecutive black squares within that row/column. Each block needs
to be separated by at least one white square.
