Guest Puzzle 1,451
How to play:
Each clue represents an expression,
or description with each capital
letter representing a word beginning
with that letter and each number
representing itself.
e.g. 1 S. S. for M.
= ‘One small step for man’
2 W. on a B.
= ‘Two wheels on a bicycle’
1 R. to R. T. A.
2 W. in a F.
3 G. make a H.
4 F., 1 T.
5 T. at H. A.
6 C. on a P. F.
7 D. S. and H. V.
8 P. in the S. S.
9 A. C. in T. P.
