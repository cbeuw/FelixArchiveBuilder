SLITHERLINK
Connect horizontally and vertically adjacent dots so that the lines form a
simple loop with no loose ends. The number inside a square represents
how many of its four sides are segments in the loop.
SHIKAKU
Divide the square into rectangles, such that each rectangle con-
tains exactly one number.The numbers given represent the area of
the containing rectangle.
