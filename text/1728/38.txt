SUDOKU
Fill all the boxes with a number between 1
and 9. A number can only appear once in a
row/column, and only once in each sub-box.
SHIKAKU
Divide the square into rectangles, such that
each rectangle contains exactly one num-
ber. The numbers given represent the area
of the containing rectangle.
WORD PYRAMID
Each row is an anagram of the previous, plus or minus one letter.
Clues: Response to cute (2) - Stack of bills (3) - Not dusk (4) -
Moon progression (5) - Guardian (6) - Bristles (5) - Provost (4) -

MINESWEEPER
The number in a cell represents the
number of mines adjacent to that square
(up/down, left/right, diagonal). Find and
mark all of the mines to clear the board.
