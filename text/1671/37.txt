Nonogram
Fill in neighbouring cells in each row/column to make
blocks. Each block is separated by one or more empty cells.
The numbers indicate the ordered lengths of the blocks to
appear in that row/column.
Slitherlink
Connect adjacent points to form a single, non-crossing loop where the num-
bers indicate how many sides of the cell are closed.
Boxes
Divide the grid into rectangles, with a single number in each, indicating the
area.
