THEFTS OF CASH AND VALUABLES
Students and staff are again requested not to leave their
jackets and brief cases containing â€¢ cash, cheque books
cheque cards, pocket calculators etc in unattended rooms
where they attract the attention of walk-in thieves.
During the last two weeks there has been a spate of this
type of offence and on one occasion four wallets were
stolen at the same time from one unattended laboratory in a
Department.
Cheque books and cheque cards should always be
seperated and never left in a situation where both could be
stolen together.
