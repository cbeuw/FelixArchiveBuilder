Photograph of the week
Tanzanian Maasai children chasing our Safari vehicle desperately crying out “maji,
maji” (Swahili for water) searching for mankind’s most basic commodity. I took
this over Summer on my 6 weeks travelling with SIFE Imperial. And yes, we gave
them water.
‘Cries of the Nomadic’ by Sahil Chugani
