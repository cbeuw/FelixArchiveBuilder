MBA SPONSORSHIP
Financial Consulting Services (FCS) is one of the fastest growing areas
of the Arthur Andersen Practice. The group provides strategic financial
and operational consulting services in a number of areas, from
business planning to profit improvement, from organisational reviews to
company valuations and litigation support.
Trainee Consultants joining the group undertake data research and
analysis within multidisciplinary FCS teams on assignments of varying
lengths. Thorough in-house training is given in business and financial
skills.
After two or three years you will have the opportunity to study for an
MBA at a top European Business School. Full financial support will be
offered to candidates who have met the Firm's expectations as Trainee
Consultants.
Arthur Andersen is seeking up to 12 outstanding graduates to join FCS
in October 1992.
