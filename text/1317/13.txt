MUSICAL THEATRE
SOCIETY presents
CHESS
7.30pm
Union Concert Hall, Beit Quad
£4.50/£6.50
Chess is an emotional, dip-
lomatic and sporting bat-

friendships stretched to the
limit. It is an incredible work
of musical theatre: with a
solid, complex and twisting
storyline woven around very
deep and interesting charac-
ters, it fuses together a wide
variety of musical styles from
orchestral to choral, rock to
pop. Tickets are available
from Union reception in Beit
Quad and on the door. For
more information, visit www.
union.ic.ac.uk/mtsoc.

CHARITY BALL
ENTERTAIN
8pm
Great Hall, Sherfield Building
£10
This Saturday’s Entertain
charity ball is a one off special
in aid of the Tsunami appeal.
Highlights include funky soul
band Our Souls, rock band
Cartel, London superclub
resident MacPherson, the
Imperial Jazz Big Band, a
variety of other specialist DJs
playing Rock, Cheese, House
and RnB, a great magician,
two huge bars and hot food.
Every penny of your ticket
price goes to the tsunami
appeal.
Get your tickets now - they
will NOT be available on the
door. For more details visit
www.entertain-event.co.uk.
