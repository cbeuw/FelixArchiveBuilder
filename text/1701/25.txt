Slitherlink
Connect horizontally and vertically adja-
cent dots so that the lines form a simple
loop with no loose ends. The number in-
side a square represents how many of its
four sides are segments in the loop.
Shikaku
Divide the square into rectangular and
square pieces such that each piece
contains exactly one number. The
numbers given represents the area of
the pieces.
